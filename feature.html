Our Café Features

Specialty Coffee Selections

Savor expertly brewed coffee, from Caramel Macchiatos to Hazelnut Lattes, crafted with premium beans and your preferred level of sweetness. Each cup is made to delight your taste buds and warm your soul in the cozy ambiance of Café Adrianne.

All Day Breakfast

Start your day with hearty plates like Longganisa with Eggs & Rice or Tapa with Eggs & Rice, cooked fresh and packed with flavor. These breakfast favorites are perfect for a comforting morning meal or a satisfying anytime treat.

Warm & Aesthetic Environment

Relax in a beautifully designed space that’s cozy, calm, and Instagram-worthy. Whether you’re catching up with friends, or simply unwinding, our café provides the perfect backdrop for comfort and creativity.

Brewed Memories

Celebrate life’s most meaningful moments with us. From weddings and birthdays to simple yet heartfelt gatherings. Café Adrianne’s intimate setting and warm atmosphere create timeless memories you’ll cherish forever.

A Taste of Forever

Our event hall blends elegance and coziness with delicious food that elevates any occasion. Ideal for engagements, anniversaries, or family milestones, it’s where celebrations feel truly special.

Live Music

End your week on a high note with live music performances that fill the café with soulful vibes. Sip on your favorite drink, enjoy good company, and let the rhythm set the mood for an unforgettable evening.
